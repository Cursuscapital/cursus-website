# Undertow Methodology Decision Log

This file documents methodology decisions made before validation outcomes
are known, so the decisions cannot be revised under time pressure when
validation results land.

## Decision DL-001: Response to v1.1 Validation Failure (committed 2026-05-11)

**Context:** v1.1 pre-registration locked at commit c69a7a3. Validation 
will run after refactor completion. The framework may pass or fail.

**Decision:** If v1.1 validation FAILS the pre-registered pass criteria, 
the response is to WITHDRAW the public Undertow framework and rebuild 
as v2 from the ground up. The response is NOT to iterate to v1.2 with 
another pre-registration cycle.

**Rationale:** Three pre-registrations in two weeks risks reading as a 
calibration crisis rather than as disciplined iteration. The discipline 
of pre-registration is preserved at the per-iteration level by the stop 
rule, but it degrades at the meta-level when iteration speed is high. 
A v2 ground-up rebuild reframes the work as deliberate redesign over 
weeks rather than as rapid retries.

**What "withdraw" means operationally:**
- Public methodology page replaced with notice: "v1 retired pending v2 
  redesign; daily dashboard remains live but should be interpreted with 
  awareness that the underlying methodology is under reconstruction."
- Daily dashboard output continues to compute but is flagged as 
  "calibration under revision."
- No new pre-registration is locked until v2 design is substantively 
  different from v1 architecture (not merely retuned thresholds).
